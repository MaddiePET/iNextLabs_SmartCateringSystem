CLIENT FEEDBACK CRITERIA

Review proposal based on:
1. Clarity
- Is the plan easy to understand?
- Are sections clearly separated?

2. Feasibility
- Can the menu be executed with available suppliers?
- Are lead times realistic?

3. Value
- Does the price fit the budget?
- Does the menu quality match the event type?

4. Professionalism
- Is the proposal suitable for a corporate or formal client?
- Are risks and recommendations clearly explained?
