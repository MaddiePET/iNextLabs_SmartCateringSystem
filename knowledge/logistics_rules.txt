LOGISTICS RULES

- Procurement should start before supplier lead time.
- ALCOHOL HANDLING: Alcohol must be transported and stored separately from food.
- SEPARATE GLASSWARE: Glassware used for alcohol must not be mixed with food-service plates.
- BAR STAFF: If alcohol is served, assign 1 dedicated bartender per 50 guests.
- Staff planning: 1 server per 20 guests for food.
- Delivery: 2 hours before event start.
- PROMPT SUPPORT: Kuala Lumpur (KL) and Selangor are primary hubs.

LOCATION RESTRICTIONS

- Catering operations currently only support West Malaysia.
- Reject or flag events located in East Malaysia or outside Malaysia.
- If the event location is outside supported areas, mark the logistics risk as HIGH.
- Supported service areas include Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, Penang, Johor, Perak, Negeri Sembilan, Melaka, and other West Malaysia states.

EVENT TIMING RULES

- Minimum booking notice is 3 days before the event date.
- Events above 200 pax require at least 7 days preparation time.
- Same-day catering requests should be flagged as HIGH RISK.
- Seafood events require at least 2 days preparation buffer.
- Weekend and public holiday events may require additional staffing preparation.
- If supplier lead times exceed available preparation time, mark the plan as HIGH RISK.
- Logistics timelines must include:
  - Procurement
  - Preparation
  - Packaging
  - Delivery
  - Final setup
