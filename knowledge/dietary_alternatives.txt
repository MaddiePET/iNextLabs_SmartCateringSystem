JAPANESE THEME SUBSTITUTIONS:
- Instead of Soy Sauce (Gluten): Use Tamari or Coconut Aminos.
- Instead of Tempura Flour (Gluten): Use Cornstarch or Rice Flour.
- Instead of Mirin (Alcohol): Use Rice Vinegar with Sugar.
- Instead of Kewpie Mayo (Egg/Dairy): Use Avocado or Vegan Mayo.

MALAYSIAN THEME SUBSTITUTIONS:
- Instead of Satay Sauce (Nuts): Use Spiced Roasted Chickpea Sauce.
- Instead of Belacan (Seafood/Allergy): Use Fermented Soy Bean (Tauco).
- Instead of Ghee (Dairy): Use Vegetable Oil or Coconut Oil.
