RISK RULEBOOK

HIGH RISK:
- Customer requests PORK or non-halal meat (STRICTLY FORBIDDEN).
- Final quote exceeds total budget.
- Menu uses ingredients with CRITICAL SHORTAGE.
- Supplier lead time exceeds event preparation time.
- Same-day or urgent booking request.

MEDIUM RISK:
- Customer requests Alcohol (Wine/Beer/Sake). 
  *Check if separate bar staff are assigned.*
- Ingredient has limited availability.
- Packaging lead time is longer than 48 hours.

LOW RISK:
- All food is Halal-certified.
- Final quote is within budget.
- Alcohol request includes separate glassware/bar setup.
