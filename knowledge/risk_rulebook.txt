RISK RULEBOOK

HIGH RISK:
- Final quote exceeds total budget.
- Menu uses ingredients with CRITICAL SHORTAGE.
- Halal menu includes alcohol or non-halal meat.
- Supplier lead time exceeds event preparation time.
- Customer requests pork, alcohol, non-halal meat, or non-halal preparation.
- Customer requests a menu that conflicts with halal-only catering policy.
- Event date is too close to supplier lead times.
- Same-day or urgent booking request.
- Event preparation window is insufficient.

MEDIUM RISK:
- Ingredient has limited availability.
- Supplier lead time is close to event date.
- No vegetarian option for mixed dietary needs.
- Packaging lead time is longer than 48 hours.

LOW RISK:
- All ingredients are available.
- Final quote is within budget.
- Halal and sustainability requirements are satisfied.
