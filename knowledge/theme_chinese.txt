THEME: Chinese Fusion
HALAL RULES: STRICTLY NO PORK. No Shaoxing wine (yellow wine) in cooking. Replace with ginger juice or Halal-certified soy sauce for depth of flavor.
DISHES: Chicken/Prawn Dim Sum, Kung Pao Tofu (dried chilies and peanuts), Wok-fried Broccoli with Garlic, Yangzhou Fried Rice (using chicken ham), Sweet and Sour Fish Fillet, Braised Mixed Mushrooms.
AUTHENTICITY: Focus on "Wok Hei" (charred flavor), ginger, scallions, and sesame oil. Use only Halal-certified oyster sauce and soy sauce.
SUBSTITUTIONS: Use Chicken Char Siu (halal) instead of traditional pork. Use cornstarch for thick, glossy sauces.
