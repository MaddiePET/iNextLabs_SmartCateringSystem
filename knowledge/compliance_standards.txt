COMPLIANCE STANDARDS

- All meat must be halal certified.
- Avoid alcohol-based sauces for halal menus.
- Check seafood sauces for non-halal ingredients.
- Provide vegetarian alternatives when dietary needs mention vegetarian.
- Prefer eco-friendly packaging where possible.
- Avoid plastic straws.
- Use cornstarch cutlery or sugarcane pulp boxes if available.

HALAL-ONLY BUSINESS RULE

- This catering system only offers halal catering options.
- Pork, alcohol, non-halal meat, and alcohol-based sauces are not supported.
- If the customer requests pork, alcohol, or non-halal items, flag the request as HIGH RISK.
- The system must suggest halal alternatives instead.
- Every final proposal should clearly state: "This proposal is prepared for halal-compliant catering."
