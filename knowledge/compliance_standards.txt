COMPLIANCE STANDARDS

CORE BUSINESS POLICY:
- We are a "Halal Food Provider with a Licensed Bar Service."
- FOOD COMPLIANCE: 100% of food ingredients must be Halal-certified.
- PORK POLICY: Strictly NO PORK. Requests for pork must be rejected/flagged HIGH RISK.
- ALCOHOL POLICY: Alcohol is PERMITTED for beverage service only.
- COOKING POLICY: Alcohol (mirin, sake, wine) is NOT to be used as an ingredient in the food itself. Use halal substitutes for cooking.

JAPANESE THEME RULES:
- Teriyaki Sauce: Must use alcohol-free Halal teriyaki sauce for cooking.
- Sake/Mirin: Permitted only as a bottled beverage served at the bar.

ALLERGEN SAFETY:
- If 'Vegetarian' is requested, ensure NO meat/seafood is in the menu. Alcohol is still permitted unless 'No Alcohol' is specifically requested by the vegetarian client.

IMPORTANT:
- If dietary restrictions conflict with the proposed menu, classify as RISK: HIGH.
- Always suggest safe alternatives.
- Clearly label allergy-sensitive menu items.

Note: 
- Seafood and Fish are naturally halal; focus on checking for alcohol in sauces (mirin/sake) for Japanese themes.
- Japanese Theme Warning: Mirin and Sake are alcohol-based and NOT Halal. Ensure the Chef specifies 'Halal-certified Mirin substitute' or 'No-alcohol Teriyaki sauce'.
